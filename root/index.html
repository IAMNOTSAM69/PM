What is UNPLUGGED?

UNPLUGGED gives fans an intimate look at the live performance world. From backstage passes to rehearsal previews, this is your all-access tour into music’s beating heart.

The Experience

Backstage Pass

Walk behind the scenes, see the soundcheck, and feel the adrenaline before showtime.

Band Rehearsals

Step into the studio and witness the preparation that makes performances unforgettable.

VIP Meet & Greet

Exclusive time with the artists you love. Candid conversations, autographs, and selfies.

On-Tour Documentary

Get access to raw behind-the-scenes tour footage and interviews never shown to the public.

Sound Engineering Zone

Sit next to the sound crew and witness how the perfect audio mix is crafted in real-time.

Stage Mic Check

Watch raw, unfiltered mic checks and warm-ups before the crowd ever hears a note.
